About KK Educational and Rural Development Society

Founded in 2015, KK Educational and Rural Development Society has been at the forefront of bridging the digital divide in rural India. We are committed to providing quality IT education and skill development programs that empower individuals and transform communities.

Our mission is to create digitally literate citizens who can contribute to India's growing economy. Through our comprehensive training programs, we have successfully trained over 5,000 students and achieved a remarkable 95% placement rate.

We are recognized by various government bodies including PMKVY, NSDC, and the Government of Andhra Pradesh for our excellence in rural education and development initiatives.
